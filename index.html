Project Overview

Problem Statement

Industrial accidents due to gas leaks, fire, and air pollution are rising in Bangladesh. Traditional safety inspection methods are time-consuming, risky, and reactive.

  • Increasing industrial accidents in Bangladesh
  • Time-consuming traditional inspection methods
  • Risky working conditions for inspectors
  • Reactive rather than proactive safety measures

Our Solution

We built a quadcopter drone equipped with gas sensors, flame sensors, and dust detectors that can fly into hazardous environments, detect issues early, and alert authorities via mobile communication.

  • Quadcopter drone with multiple sensors
  • Early detection of hazards
  • Mobile communication for alerts
  • Access to hazardous environments

Objectives

Our drone-based system is engineered to identify fire outbreaks and gas leakage spots in real-time, providing a rapid response advantage in critical situations. It is equipped to monitor optical dust particles in industrial environments, ensuring better visibility into air quality and safety risks.

Identify fire and gas leakage spots in real time

Real-time detection and localization of hazards for immediate response.

Send immediate alerts to operator and fire station

Automated notification system for rapid emergency response.

Monitor optical dust particles

Continuous air quality monitoring in industrial environments.

Assist in industrial inspections in high-risk areas

Reducing human risk in dangerous inspection scenarios.
